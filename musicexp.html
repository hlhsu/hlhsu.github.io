Music

1st stand of viola in Tsing Hua Symphony Orchestra

I'm a music lover and I have practiced the viola and piano since I was 4 years old. Because of my abundant performing and training experiences in playing the viola, I was selected as the first stand of viola in Tsing Hua Symphony Orchestra for 4 years in college and performed more than 20 concerts on public stages such as National Concert Hall.

Recent music experiences and honors:
  • Music competitions (piano): Winner of NTHU concerto competition (2017)
  • 1st stand of viola in NTHU Symphony Orchestra (2014-2018)
  • 1st prize of viola solo in The National Music Contest Finals (2011).
  • 1st prize of piano quintet in The National Music Contest Finals (2011).
  • Composer of the graduation song of Zhong-Shan Girl Senior High School (2014).
  • Piano accompanist of the Choir in Zhong-Shan Girl Senior High School (2011-2014)

    • Piano concerto soloist, Annual summer concert of NTHU orchestra

    I won the National Tsing Hua University concerto competition in 2017 and I performed Rachmaninoff's Piano Concerto No 2 which is one of the most popular and recognisable concertos in the classical repertoire at our school's auditorium. Click to see my video:   Youtube Link

    Student Volunteer

    I really like children. Hence, I served as a Student Volunteer to launch a Musical Charity Camp for countryside elementary schools. In this event, we taught those children to play the string insturments.

    Violaist in NTHU AI Orchestra

    As a music lover with computer science background, I joined the NTHU Artificial Intelligence (AI) Orchestra during my graduate years, performing the music generated by Artificial Intelligence Virtual Artist (AIVA), the most well-known electronic composer.

    Leadership

    Team Manager, NTHU table tennis team

    I joined NTHU table tennis team and served as team manager in my college life for 2 years. I performed management duties such as supporting the coach with matters relating to the team, organizing equipment for team practices and games, taking photos and preparing foods for team memebers during the competition. During the second year I stayed in the team, we won the table tennis competition in Mei-Chu Tournament which is an annual sports competition between National Tsing Hua University (NTHU) and National Chiao Tung University (NCTU).

    Leader of Team Mentors, NTHU CS Camp

    In 2016, I served as Leader of Team Mentors by leading 20 mentors to train 100 participated senior high school students in the CS Camp. The CS Camp is an annual event launched by undergraduate students from NTHU CS to train senior high school students with basic computer science knowledge so as to motivate them to study computer science.

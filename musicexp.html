Music

1st stand of viola in Tsing Hua Symphony Orchestra

I'm a music lover and I have practiced the viola and piano for more than 15 years. Because of my abundant performing and training experiences in playing the viola, I was selected as the first stand of viola in Tsing Hua Symphony Orchestra for 4 years in college and performed more than 20 concerts on public stages such as National Concert Hall.

Recent music experiences:
  • Music competitions (piano): Winner of NTHU concerto competition (2017)
  • 1st prize of viola solo in The National Music Contest Finals (2011).
  • 1st prize of piano quintet in The National Music Contest Finals (2011).
  • Composer of the graduation song of Zhong-Shan Girl Senior High School (2014).
  • Piano accompanist of the Choir in Zhong-Shan Girl Senior High School (2011-2014)

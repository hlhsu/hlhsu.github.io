About Me

Treat learning as a journey

Intern in Microsoft

During the senior year in my college life, I grasped an internship opportunity at Microsoft Azure IoT team for one year to be a research and development (RD) engineer and to learn cloud technologies leveraging on remote edge devices. In the internship, I worked on helping to promote IoT solutions to 10+ business partner and built POC (Covers Smart City/Retail/Home/Manufacturing) leveraging Azure IoT Cloud and Edge computing services. For example, we design and build a high integration visual platform for industrial production lines with standard protocol OPCUA which can exchange information among machines from different companies.

2017 IoT EXPO

I served as a 2017 IoT Expo/Booth Tour Technical Conductor to introduce Microsoft IoT technologies with 20+ partner showcases to IoT Expo global visitors. There were 17 countries, 384 enterprises and up to 20 talks related to new technologies and concepts on site. During the two-day event, the number of the participants totaled more than 1700 persons.

Microsoft 2017 Coding Angel Bootcamp

I am selected as the technical teaching leader of Microsoft 2017 Coding Angel bootcamp which is one of the largest coding workshops for girls in Taiwan. I help to design/create Azure Machine Learning hands-on materials with Microsoft MVP and train other RD interns to deliver the workshop in the event. The number of the attendence totaled more than 100+ college girl students.

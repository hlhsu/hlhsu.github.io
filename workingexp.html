Intern in Microsoft

Empowering every person and every organization on the planet to achieve more.

During the senior year in my college life, I grasped an internship opportunity at Microsoft Azure IoT team for one year to be a Software Engineer Intern (SDE) and to learn cloud technologies leveraging on remote edge devices. In the internship, I worked on helping to promote IoT solutions to 10+ business partner and built POC (Covers Smart City/Retail/Home/Manufacturing) leveraging Azure IoT Cloud and Edge computing services. For example, we design and build a high integration visual platform for industrial production lines with standard protocol OPCUA which can exchange information among machines from different companies. Besides, I quarterly perform hands-on updated documents of IoT workshops and provide technical support of Azure to those Microsoft’s engineering partners.

2017 IoT EXPO

I served as a 2017 IoT Expo/Booth Tour Technical Conductor to introduce Microsoft IoT technologies with 20+ partner showcases to IoT Expo global visitors. There were 17 countries, 384 enterprises and up to 20 talks related to new technologies and concepts on site. During the two-day event, the number of the participants totaled more than 1700 persons.

Microsoft 2017 Coding Angel Bootcamp

I was selected as the technical teaching leader of Microsoft 2017 Coding Angel bootcamp which is one of the largest coding workshops for girls in Taiwan. I help to design/create Azure Machine Learning hands-on materials with Microsoft Data Science MVP and train other RD interns to deliver the workshop in the event. The number of the attendences totaled more than 100+ college girl students. During my intership, I also eared the Microsoft Data Science Certificate from Microsoft Professional Program (MPP) which is a 6 months training track.

Microsoft

Empowering every person and every organization on the planet to achieve more.

Research Intern, AI + Cloud Engineering Group

During the senior year in my college life, I grasped an internship opportunity at Microsoft Cloud and AI Engineering Group for one year to be a Research Engineer Intern and to learn cloud technologies leveraging on remote edge devices. In the internship, I worked on helping to promote IoT solutions to 10+ business partner and built POC (Covers Smart City/Retail/Home/Manufacturing) leveraging Azure Cloud and Edge computing services. For example, I designed and built a high integration visual platform for industrial production lines with standard protocol OPCUA which can exchange information among machines from different companies. Besides, I quarterly performed hands-on updated documents of technical workshops and provide support of Azure to those Microsoft’s engineering partners.

Abbott Laboratories

Software Engineering Intern, Abbott Vascular Taiwan

At the end of my undergraduate studies, I was recommended by my advisor, Prof. Hao-Ming Hsiao to become a Software Engineering Intern at Abbott Vascular Taiwan. I worked full-time and part-time for 13 months. In addition to improving the operation time in the sales database of vascular products, I also develop a new administrative system to share information among the marketing, sales, and finance departments. Eventually, my colleagues are able to forecast vascular product marketing trends by digitizing routine documents and I also incorporated the original database with Power BI to provide interactive visualizations and business intelligence capabilities to create reports and dashboards.

It was my pleasure to work with those supportive mentors and colleagues, and We have a lively atmosphere in the office. I completed my internship journey at Abbott by giving a presentation to the general manager at Abbott Vascular Taiwan. I researched the design, simulation, and manufacturing of stents in the lab. I was fortunate to also get familiar with vascular products from different perspectives during this internship.

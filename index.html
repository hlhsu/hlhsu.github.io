About Me

I’m Yun-Yun (Alice) Tsai (蔡昀芸), a research assistant in the Institute of Computer Science at National Tsing Hua University (NTHU), Taiwan. I received M.S. and B.S. in computer science, both from NTHU. My research interests lie in Security and Artificial Intelligence, which I am particularly interests in improving trustworthy, security, and robustness over machine learning (ML) algorithms and computer systems. Currently, I work on Limited-data machine learning and Robustness of neural network with Professor Tsung-Yi Ho and Dr. Pin-Yu Chen from IBM Research Trusted AI group. In my free time, I love to play music and I'm talented at playing the piano and viola.

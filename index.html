About Me

  • I’m Hao-Lun (Howard) Hsu, and I am a graduate research assistant at Emory University, advised by Prof. Mahmoudi Babak.
  • My research interests lie at the intersection of Reinforcement Learning and Robotics/ Neuromodulation, which I am particularly interested in improving safety, interpretability, and robustness with real-world data.
  • I received M.S. in Biomedical Engineering from Georgia Institute of Technology (Georgia Tech) and B.S. in Mechanical Engineering from National Taiwan University (NTU). I worked with Prof Sehoon Ha on developing safe Reinforcement Learning algorithms during my master's studies.

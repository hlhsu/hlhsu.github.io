Hao-Lun (Howard) Hsu

hhsu61@gatech.edu
I am currently a second-year master's student with dual majors in Biomedical Engineering and Computational Science & Engineering at Georgia Institute of Technology, advised by Dr. Sehoon Ha. I received a B.S in Mechanical Engineering from National Taiwan University working with Dr. Hao-Ming Hsiao. My research interest lies at the intersection between Artifical Intilligence and Network Science, with a focus on the applications of reinforcement learning in power system and neuroscience. I have previously worked at Abbott Vascular as a Software Engineer Intern.
